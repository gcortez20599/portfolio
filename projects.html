My Projects

Coffee Shop Website

For this project, my plan was to create a website that would reflect the unique vibe of Fantasias Cafe, a Latina-owned coffee shop. I aimed to design a user-friendly platform that captured the essence of the cafe’s brand. My approach involved choosing colors and design elements that would evoke a welcoming atmosphere. The website needed to showcase the cafe's offerings and ensure that customers could easily navigate the menu. My focus was on creating a seamless experience for visitors, ensuring the site would be accessible and visually engaging while supporting the business's growth.

DJ Couple Portfolio

This project aimed to develop a website that would help my parents, DJs specializing in house music, grow their business and connect with their audience. I envisioned a site that would not only serve as an online portfolio but also allow fans and clients to easily book them for events. The website needed to reflect their unique music style while offering a smooth navigation experience. I focused on creating a layout that would display their work, products, and services in a way that would be easy for visitors to access and interact with, while also ensuring the design was aligned with their brand.

Adobe MAX Animation

For the Adobe MAX event, my plan was to design a set of visual elements that would enhance the overall event experience.
